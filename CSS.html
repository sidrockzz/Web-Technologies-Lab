Quantum Computing

The Revolutionary Technology

Quantum Computing is the use of quantum-mechanical phenomena such as superposition and entanglement to perform computation.

Qubits are fundamental to quantum computing and are somewhat analogous to bits in a classical computer. Qubits can be in a 1 or 0 quantum state. But they can also be in a superposition of the 1 and 0 states.
